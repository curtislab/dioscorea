Significance

Yams have shared both an economic and cultural significance for millenia as both a food resource and cash crop. Historically, it has been traded all over the world; hence the numerous global varieties in most continents (i.e. all but Antartica). It has also been used throughout cultural traditions including youth initiation, payment of dowry, and other rites. It remains a staple nutritional resource, providing 200 dietary calories to over 300M people. Some varities even hold significance in tribal medical practices.
